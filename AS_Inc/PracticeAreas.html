As a business orientated practice, we are able to offer our corporate client’s focused legal attention and our individual client’s will benefit from this in that we offer our macro commercial expertise and focus to their personal legal needs. 

Along with our focus areas of practice we provide general legal services such as mediation and arbitration, litigation, debt collection, family law, liquidations, immigration law, property law, insolvencies and general commercial law. 

We at all times analyse the legal risks posed to our clients and advise them on the best and most cost effective route forward in their legal matters. 

We are able to advise on business, legal strategies and various courses of action to take during decision making sessions. 

We report regularly to our Clients and provide detailed feedback in a user-friendly format on the progress of their matters. 

Through our network across the country we are in a position to assist in a variety of jurisdictions. 

Aside from private clients, we also act as service providers to other law firms as Legal Cost Consultants and Correspondents in the Randburg and Johannesburg Courts.
