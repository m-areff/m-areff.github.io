About Us

AS Inc. Attorneys was formed to provide a niche personalised service to Clients.

Afzel Seedat, Attorney and Director of AS Inc. Attorneys has over 10 years of diverse legal experience, having been admitted as an Attorney with Right of Appearance in the High Court of South Africa in 2010.

His experience ranges from acting for and against parastatals, government, medium and large corporate entities, individuals and families ranging from corporate commercial litigation to contracts and a variety of legal services.

He has successfully represented individuals, companies and has been involved in many major matters in South Africa. He has also been involved in a number of matters which have become reported case law.

AS Inc. Attorneys is a full service law firm that specialises in general litigation services, deceased estates, insolvency law, company law, family law, labour law and property law.

Our Mission is to provide cost effective services to our Clients. 

Vision

We strive to continue to be a reliable, trustworthy and dependable firm, a beacon that our Clients can turn to. We wish to maintain a successful partnership with our Clients that over time will grow. We aim to become synonymous with dependability, consistency and Client satisfaction.

Mission

The mission of AS Inc. Attorneys is to provide our Clients with conscientious legal assistance. By provided continuous feedback and interacting consistently with our Clients, we are able to ensure their understanding of the legal process.
